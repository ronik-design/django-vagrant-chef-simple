HOMELAB Homesurvey Browser

Homesurvey Browser is a Django project for analysis of survey data. This website allows researchers to pull in data from LimeSurvey and perform analysis by finding survey participants meeting certain qualifications, and then extract specific information for the selected participants.

All of these features require a login.

About Homelab

The Georgia Tech HomeLab provides an independent evaluation capability to measure the long-term effectiveness and suitability of home health and wellness products in real-life settings.
